When you open your first pull request, the CLA assistant bot will ask you to
sign. You sign by adding your name and email to the comment. One signature
covers all future contributions.

If you are contributing on behalf of your employer, please have an authorized
representative sign the Entity CLA by contacting licensing@usedicta.com.

## Why this matters

The Librarian uses a dual-license model (AGPL-3.0 + Commercial). The CLA
ensures that every line of code in the project can be distributed under both
licenses. Without it, contributions would be locked to AGPL-only, fragmenting
the codebase and limiting what we can ship to commercial users.

We want contributors to feel good about this arrangement. Your work stays
yours, the open-source edition stays free, and the commercial edition funds
continued development of the project.
